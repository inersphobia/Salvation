# Septagram timer. 

You can run this on Mac, iOS, or Android. 

For iOS (using Safari):
1. click the Share button.
2. Click "Add To Home Screen".
3. Launch the app from your homescreen.

For Android (using Chrome):
1. Click the three dots at the top right of your screen.
2. Click "Add to Home Screen".
3. Launch the app from your homescreen.

For MacOS (using Safari):
1. Click the Share button.
2. click "Add to Dock".
3. Launch the application.

How to use:
1. Tap the clock to start. Tap again to pause, and again to resume.
2. MODE cycles between 27, 54, and 81 minute sessions.
3. RESET clears the current session.
3b. VIEW cycles the display: STAR (septagram), RINGS (concentric orbits), ABACUS (7 rows of 3 cells), and FRACTAL (Sierpinski descent). All four show the same base-3 time; you can switch mid-session.
4. The digits show minutes and thirds (1/3, 1/9, 1/27 of a minute) in base 3; the three-box incrementer below the clock always steps 3x faster than the top (fastest) triangle. It is hidden in 27-minute mode, where it would tick too fast to read.
5. After a cycle completes, overtime counts up until you tap to restart or press RESET.
