# Septagram timer. 

You can run this on Mac, iOS, or Android. 

For iOS (using Safari):
1. click the Share button.
2. Click "Add To Home Screen".
3. Launch the app from your homescreen.

For Android (using Chrome):
1. Click the three dots at the top right of your screen.
2. Click "Add to Home Screen".
3. Launch the app from your homescreen.

For MacOS (using Safari):
1. Click the Share button.
2. click "Add to Dock".
3. Launch the application.

How to use:
1. Change the number of hops on the path by clicking the green button at the bottom right. You can choose to only show only corners, or should halfway betwen corners.
2. Choose 81 minute mode or 27 minute mode.
3. Start
4. Pause
5. Reset
6. Finish early
